About School Flourish


The School Flourish is a non-profit organisation with a mission to build the largest network of innovative schools that empowers unprivileged children to maximise their potential and that influences systemic reform. School Flourish has been taken to educate children from low-income communities.
Looking ahead, we seek to expand our network of online schooling , share our effective practices and advocate for quality online schooling reform forunprivileged children across Indonesia.

In the short term

School Flourish aims to redefine what is possible for children from low-income communities to achieve through its network of high-performing public-private partnership schools.

In the long term

We will share our model and practices through partnerships with the government and other organizations.We aim to leverage our demonstrated impact to advocate for reforms with other like-minded organizations that will lead to an excellent education for all children.Our rigors and updated course curriculum helps students to acquire knowledge, skills and attitude to fulfill the ever- changing requirements of the corporate world





Eligibility For Admission

A student seeking admission to any class will be eligible for admission
to that class only if he/she:


1. Income must be less then 1,965,000 IDR.


2.Must have passed in all the subject(If he/she had been
to any institution previously.


3. Satisfies the requirements of age limits (minimum and
maximum) as determined by the government.














Document Required


1. Birth Certificate


2. Migration Certificate (original required) if the candidate has been studied any institute.


3. Income Certificate,


4. 10 Photographs (Colored).


5. 3 Sets of duly attested photocopies of all applicable documents by a Gazetted Officer
(original documents are required only for verification, after verification, originals shall be returned back).


6. Community Certificate (Physically Handicapped).


7. Certificate of medical fitness.
